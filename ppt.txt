SLIDE 2 — PROBLEM STATEMENT
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The Problem We Are Solving:

• A large segment of the population — especially elderly people and those with
  chronic conditions — cannot access a doctor whenever they need one.

• People cannot understand their own prescriptions, lab reports, or test results
  because they are written in medical/clinical language.

• Patients lose prescriptions, forget medication history, and cannot communicate
  their complete treatment history to new doctors — leading to repeated tests,
  inefficient consultations, and fragmented care.

• There is no structured system for patients to track their medical journey
  over time in one place.

• Mental and emotional well-being is largely ignored — there are no
  easily accessible tools for everyday emotional support or mood tracking.

Core Insight:
  People need a trusted, always-available health companion that can guide them
  between doctor visits — not replace the doctor, but help them understand,
  track, and manage their health better.

SLIDE 3 — WHAT IS AAROGYAN?
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Aarogyan is a cross-platform mobile health application (Android & iOS) that acts
as an AI-powered personal health companion.

What it does (in simple terms):
  1. Lets users manage their full medical profile in one place
  2. Tracks every doctor visit and medical consultation over time
  3. Chats with users about health questions using AI
  4. Reads and explains prescriptions and lab reports in plain language
  5. Provides a friendly AI voice companion for emotional support
  6. Tracks mood and mental well-being trends over time
  7. Exports complete consultation history as a shareable PDF

Important: Aarogyan is a PRECAUTIONARY and ASSISTIVE tool.
           It does NOT diagnose diseases or prescribe medicines.
           It helps users understand information and prepare better.

SLIDE 5 — FEATURE 1: CONSULTATION TRACKER
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it is:
  A digital medical diary where users can track every treatment episode
  across multiple doctor visits — all in one place.

How it works:
  • User creates a named Consultation (e.g., "Knee Pain — July 2025")
  • Under each Consultation, they add individual Sessions (one per doctor visit)
  • Each Session stores:
      - Date of visit
      - Symptoms reported
      - Diagnosis given
      - Medications prescribed
      - Doctor's notes
      - Uploaded documents (prescriptions, lab reports, scans)
  • All sessions are displayed in a chronological timeline view
  • User can view, edit, or add to any session at any time

Document Upload:
  • Upload PDF, JPG, or PNG files (max 2 MB per file)
  • Files are attached to the specific session they belong to
  • Stored securely in cloud storage

PDF Export:
  • Generate a complete, formatted PDF report of the entire consultation
  • Includes all session data + embedded documents
  • Can be downloaded and shared with any doctor

Why it matters:
  Patients often lose track of their medical history. This feature ensures
  nothing is forgotten — every visit is recorded, and everything can be
  shared instantly with a new doctor.

SLIDE 6 — FEATURE 2: MEDICAL ASSISTANT (AI HEALTH CHAT)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it is:
  A conversational AI chat assistant that answers health questions in a
  warm, plain-language, non-clinical way — personalised to the user's profile.

How it works:
  • Every time the user opens the Assistant, a fresh new conversation starts
  • User types (or eventually speaks) a health question
  • The AI responds with helpful, general health information
  • Full conversation history is saved automatically after every exchange
  • Users can access, re-read, or continue any past conversation at any time
  • Users can delete any conversation permanently

What makes it smart:
  • The user's full medical profile (conditions, allergies, medications,
    lifestyle) is passed to the AI in the background — so responses are
    personalised to that specific person
  • Uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) — the AI searches a trusted
    medical knowledge base before answering complex questions
  • Automatically routes simple questions to a fast model and complex
    questions through the full knowledge retrieval pipeline
  • Supports English, Hindi, and Marathi — detects the user's language
    automatically and responds in the same language

What it will NOT do:
  • Will not diagnose any condition definitively
  • Will not recommend or prescribe specific medicines or dosages
  • Will not answer anything outside health, wellness, and nutrition topics

SLIDE 7 — FEATURE 3: DOCUMENT SUMMARISATION (AI OCR)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it is:
  A tool that reads any medical document (prescription, lab report, scan)
  and explains it to the user in simple, friendly language.

How it works:
  Step 1 — User uploads a file (PDF, JPG, or PNG, max 1.5 MB)
  Step 2 — OCR (Optical Character Recognition) extracts all the text,
            even from handwritten prescriptions
  Step 3 — The AI analyses the extracted text
  Step 4 — A structured summary is returned to the user, containing:
              - Document type (Prescription / Blood Report / Lab Report / etc.)
              - Plain-language explanation of what the document means
              - List of key findings (medicines, abnormal values, instructions)
              - Confidence score (how clearly the document was read)
              - Medical disclaimer

OCR Technology Used:
  EasyOCR — open-source, handles handwriting and multiple fonts.
  PyMuPDF — renders PDF pages as high-resolution images for better accuracy.

Why it matters:
  Most patients have no way to understand what their lab report or prescription
  says. This feature translates complex medical language into something anyone
  can understand — instantly, for free.

SLIDE 8 — FEATURE 4: EMOTIONAL BUDDY (ORBZ)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it is:
  An animated AI companion named "Orbz" that provides warm, non-clinical
  emotional support through voice or text conversations.

How it works:
  • User opens the Orbz screen — sees the animated companion
  • User can speak to Orbz (voice input) or type their feelings
  • Orbz responds empathetically with supportive, carefully worded messages
  • Orbz also speaks back (voice output) in a warm, human-sounding voice
  • The session's mood data is recorded for the Mental Health Tracker

What makes it intelligent:
  • Speech-to-Text: User's spoken words are converted to text using
    Groq Whisper (fast, accurate transcription)
  • Text-to-Speech: Orbz's responses are spoken aloud using Sarvam AI's
    TTS engine with 12 voice options (male and female, Indian voices)
  • Emotion Detection (Text): A DistilRoBERTa ML model analyses the
    user's text and detects their emotional tone
  • Emotion Detection (Audio): A Wav2Vec2 ML model analyses the user's
    voice tone independently of the words spoken
  • Fusion Engine: Both text and audio emotion signals are intelligently
    combined to determine the user's true emotional state
  • Language support: English, Hindi, and Marathi

Voice Options (12 available):
  Female voices: Priya (Warm & gentle), Simran (Calm & soothing),
                 Kavya (Soft & empathetic), Shreya, Neha, Roopa
  Male voices:   Aditya (Calm & reassuring), Kabir (Deep & grounding),
                 Anand, Rohan (Friendly & steady), Dev, Rahul

Why it matters:
  Emotional health is as important as physical health. Orbz gives users a
  judgment-free space to express how they feel — available 24/7.

SLIDE 9 — FEATURE 5: MENTAL HEALTH TRACKER
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it is:
  A visual dashboard that shows the user's emotional and mood trends
  over time — automatically built from their Orbz sessions.

What the dashboard shows:
  • Most recent session mood (hero card at the top)
  • Daily, weekly, and monthly mood trend charts
  • Emotion distribution (how often the user felt happy, sad, angry, neutral)
  • Emotional volatility & stability score
  • Session-by-session breakdown of emotional intensity
  • An AI-generated "insight summary" sentence describing the overall trend
    (e.g., "Your mood has shown a consistent upward trend this week")

How the data flows:
  Every time a user talks to Orbz, the detected emotions from that session
  are stored. The Mental Health Tracker aggregates all this data and
  renders it as easy-to-read visual charts.

Emotion Labels Tracked:
  HAPPY  —  NEUTRAL  —  SAD  —  ANGRY

Why it matters:
  Users cannot notice their own emotional patterns without seeing them over
  time. This tracker makes invisible mood changes visible, helping users
  take action before they reach a crisis point.

SLIDE 10 — FEATURE 6: USER HEALTH PROFILE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

What it is:
  A comprehensive personal medical profile that every user fills out once
  and updates as needed. This profile powers personalised AI responses.

What is stored in the profile:
  Personal Information:
    Full name, date of birth, biological sex, blood group, height, weight,
    contact info, emergency contact

  Medical Information:
    - Known conditions (with severity and year of diagnosis)
    - Allergies (type, reaction, severity)
    - Current medications (dosage, frequency, route, prescribed for)
    - Supplements
    - Past medical & surgical history
    - Family history of conditions

  Lifestyle Information:
    - Activity level, exercise type & frequency
    - Dietary preference & eating patterns
    - Sleep hours & sleep issues
    - Smoking, alcohol, tobacco use
    - Stress level

  Mental Health Information:
    - Diagnosed mental health conditions
    - Current mental health medications
    - Whether in therapy

How it is used:
  The user's profile is invisibly passed to the Medical Assistant AI before
  every response — so the AI can give relevant, personal answers without
  the user needing to re-explain themselves every time.

SLIDE 12 — TECHNOLOGY STACK
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Frontend (Mobile App):
  • Flutter + Dart               — Cross-platform (Android + iOS) from one codebase
  • Riverpod (code-gen)          — State management
  • GoRouter                     — Navigation and auth-guard routing
  • Material 3 Design            — Custom teal-based design system
  • DM Sans font                 — Clean, modern, accessible typography
  • Dio + JWT interceptor        — Secure API communication

Backend (API Server):
  • Python + FastAPI             — High-performance REST API
  • Pydantic                     — Data validation and schema definition
  • Hosted on HuggingFace Spaces — Free cloud deployment

Database & Storage:
  • Supabase                     — PostgreSQL database + file storage (cloud)
  • JWT Authentication           — Secure user sessions

AI / ML Services:
  • Groq (LLaMA / DeepSeek)     — Main LLM for Medical Assistant & Buddy responses
  • RAG Pipeline                  — Retrieval-Augmented Generation for accurate answers
    ↳ Qdrant Cloud               — Vector database for medical knowledge search
    ↳ BAAI/bge-small-en-v1.5    — Sentence embedding model
    ↳ CrossEncoder (MiniLM)     — Re-ranking model for better search results

OCR:
  • EasyOCR                      — Text extraction (handles handwriting)
  • PyMuPDF (fitz)               — PDF-to-image rendering

Voice (Speech):
  • Groq Whisper (large-v3-turbo) — Speech-to-Text (fast, accurate)
  • Sarvam AI TTS (bulbul:v3)    — Text-to-Speech (Indian voices, 3 languages)

Emotion Detection:
  • j-hartmann/emotion-english-distilroberta-base  — Text emotion model
  • superb/wav2vec2-base-superb-er                — Audio/voice emotion model
  • Custom Fusion Engine          — Combines text + audio emotion signals

SLIDE 14 — SECURITY & PRIVACY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

• JWT (JSON Web Token) based authentication — tokens expire for security
• All passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage
• All API endpoints require valid authentication — no data is public
• Row-level data isolation — users can only see their own data
• Files stored in private Supabase storage buckets
• HTTPS enforced for all API communication (backend on HTTPS host)
• CORS configured to only allow requests from the app
• File upload validation — type and size limits enforced server-side
• No diagnosis, no prescription — AI is scoped strictly to safe responses
• Built following OWASP security principles

SLIDE 15 — AI INTELLIGENCE HIGHLIGHTS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Smart Query Routing:
  Every health question is first classified by an LLM classifier before
  being answered. Simple questions are answered directly. Complex questions
  go through the full RAG pipeline (search medical knowledge base first,
  then generate a grounded answer). This makes responses faster and more
  accurate.

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation):
  Instead of relying purely on what the LLM already knows, the system
  first searches a curated medical knowledge vector database (Qdrant Cloud)
  for the most relevant medical information, then feeds that context to
  the LLM to generate a well-grounded answer.

Multimodal Emotion Fusion:
  Orbz detects emotions from BOTH what the user says (text) and how they
  say it (voice tone). A deterministic fusion engine combines both signals
  intelligently — for example, if the text sounds neutral but the voice
  suggests sadness, the system trusts the voice more. This gives a much
  more accurate picture of the user's emotional state than text alone.

Personalised Responses:
  The user's full medical profile is serialised into plain text and injected
  into every Medical Assistant conversation — meaning the AI knows the
  user's conditions, allergies, and medications automatically and responds
  with context-aware guidance.

Session Analytics:
  After every Orbz session, the system automatically computes:
  emotional trend (start/middle/end), volatility (how much the mood changed),
  stability score (0–100), and generates a human-readable insight summary.

SLIDE 20 — BACKEND API ENDPOINTS (TECHNICAL SLIDE)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Authentication:
  POST  /api/v1/auth/signup           — Register new user
  POST  /api/v1/auth/login            — Login, receive JWT token

Profile:
  GET   /api/v1/profile/me            — Get user profile
  PUT   /api/v1/profile/me            — Update/create user profile

Consultation Tracker:
  GET   /api/v1/consultations/        — List all consultations
  POST  /api/v1/consultations/        — Create new consultation
  GET   /api/v1/consultations/{id}    — Get single consultation
  PATCH /api/v1/consultations/{id}    — Update consultation
  DELETE/api/v1/consultations/{id}    — Delete consultation

Sessions (within a consultation):
  GET   /api/v1/consultations/{id}/sessions/      — List sessions
  POST  /api/v1/consultations/{id}/sessions/      — Add new session
  POST  /api/v1/consultations/{id}/sessions/{id}/documents  — Upload file

Medical Assistant:
  GET   /api/v1/assistant/conversations           — List conversations
  POST  /api/v1/assistant/conversations           — Start new conversation
  GET   /api/v1/assistant/conversations/{id}      — Get conversation + messages
  POST  /api/v1/assistant/chat                    — Send message, get AI reply
  DELETE/api/v1/assistant/conversations/{id}      — Delete conversation

Document Summarisation:
  POST  /api/v1/documents/summarise   — Upload file, get OCR + summary

Emotional Buddy:
  POST  /api/v1/buddy/text            — Send text, get Orbz response + emotion
  POST  /api/v1/buddy/voice           — Send audio, get Orbz response + transcript
  POST  /api/v1/buddy/tts             — Convert text to speech
  GET   /api/v1/buddy/voices          — List available voice options

Mental Health Tracker:
  GET   /api/v1/mental-health/dashboard  — Get mood charts and analytics

Export:
  GET   /api/v1/export/consultation/{id}/pdf  — Download full consultation PDF

Hosted at:  https://sg2407-aarogyan.hf.space/api/v1
